# iOS 26 Motion Design

## Timings
- Fast: 150ms
- Standard: 200ms
- Emphasized: 250ms
- Elastic: 300ms (cubic elastic)

## Easing
- Standard: `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0.0, 0.2, 1)`
- Elastic: `cubic-bezier(0.68, -0.55, 0.265, 1.55)`

## Springs (Framer)
- Gentle: `damping: 20, stiffness: 180`
- Snappy: `damping: 24, stiffness: 240`

- Hover: scale `1.02`, brightness `+10%`

- Focus: outline `2px`, offset `2px`
