# iOS 26 Asset Style Guide

## Icons
- SVG strokes: 1.5–2px, rounded caps and joins
- Grid alignment: 8pt keylines; center and baseline alignment

## Images & Vectors
- Variants: `@1x/@2x/@3x` resolution independent
- Avoid inner glows; use minimal drop shadows consistent with depth layer

## Treatments
- No heavy frosting; blur `≤0.5px`
- Edge glare allowed for pill highlights; blend modes `soft-light` when needed
